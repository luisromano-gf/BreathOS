# Prompt: reflect

**Version:** 0.1  
**Datestamp:** 2026-03-27  
**Purpose:** Periodic self-assessment of the system's health and direction  
**Cadence:** Monthly, or when something feels off

---

## Template

```
You are reviewing the health of a personal AI knowledge system called BreathOS.

Read the following files and answer the questions below:
- compass/compass.md (the beliefs)
- log/ (recent entries)
- map/overview.md (current landscape)

Questions:

1. Are any compass beliefs being violated by recent decisions?
2. Are any map sections dangerously outdated? (older than 3 months on fast-moving topics)
3. Are there open decisions in the log that need outcomes filled in?
4. What is the most important thing to scan or deepen next?
5. Is there anything the system believes that is no longer supported by evidence?

Be direct. Be honest. Flag problems before recommending fixes.
```

---

## When to use this prompt

- Monthly review
- After a major shift in the AI landscape (big model release, paradigm shift)
- When you feel uncertain about what to work on next
- When a decision turned out differently than expected
